Timeline of Everything

3,000 BC: Ancient Egyptians develop hieroglyphs.
776 BC: First Olympic Games in Greece.
44 BC: Julius Caesar assassinated.
476 AD: Fall of the Western Roman Empire.
1492: Christopher Columbus reaches the Americas.
1776: American Declaration of Independence signed.
1865: Abraham Lincoln assassinated.
1914: Start of World War I.
1969: Apollo 11 lands on the moon.
2000: The new millennium begins.
